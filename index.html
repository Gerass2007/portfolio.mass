About me

Intro

My name is Anastasiya (for friends Nastya).

Was born in 1999 in Minsk, Belarus. Studies Journalism in BSU but didn’t work a day in this field.

At the age of 14 when I outgrew all my ballroom dancing partners my mom sent me to a model school. I didn’t graduate from it because I quit after 2 months. But accidently after a few months the makeup artist found my social page and asked to come as a model for beauty shooting. Of course I agreed. And further along the chain other photographers began to call me on shootings.

To be honest I was pretty popular at my 15 in Belarus and was even invited to the Moscow agency Grace. But I was a little girl of her parents. Plus studying at school and have great studying plans for the future. So worked only on holidays and weekends and only in Russia. Then I grew up and stated working all around the world. From that moment I took part in more than 1000 castings!

I got into acting a little later. I think around 2019. Just found some groups with castings and took on several roles. I love this field so much, this dynamism and energy, love camera and the creative people behind it.
